September COTM Bullets
Bullets are due 30 SEP

As a reminder, September Cadet of the Month Bullets are due 30 SEP by COB. Pass up your bullets to your one up. If you have any questions, please ask your chain of command.

Marathon Water Station Volunteers

The virtual AF Marathon takes place on 26 SEP. Volunteers are needed to man the water stations, and pass out water to the runners. If you're interested, please sign up here.

Concessions & Parking Volunteer Sign-ups

If you wish to volunteer to help out concessions or parking during football games, please fill out the concessions spreadsheet here, or the parking spreadsheet here. Volunteering counts for Honor Flight points. This is not mandatory and all cadets are encouraged to volunteer.

Operation Jet Pack

Help clean up the stadium the day after games using leaf blowers. This is a great way to take the initiative and put your name out there and earn double Honor Flight Points, however, please note this is not mandatory. Sign up here.
